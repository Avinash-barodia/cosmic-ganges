Frequently asked questions.

What happens during a session?

Every session begins with a conversation about your concerns, intentions, and goals. Depending on the service chosen, you will be gently guided through a healing, exploratory, or intuitive process designed to help you gain clarity, healing, and insight.

Do I need any prior spiritual experience?

Not at all. Many clients are completely new to spiritual healing. You don't need special knowledge, beliefs, or experience - just an open mind and a willingness to explore.

Are sessions available online?

Yes. Sessions are available both online and in person. Clients from around the world have experienced meaningful results through virtual sessions conducted in a safe and comfortable environment.

How do I know which service is right for me?

If you're unsure, that's perfectly okay. During a brief consultation, we'll discuss your situation and recommend the healing modality that best aligns with your current needs and goals.

How long does a session typically last?

Most sessions last between 60 to 90 minutes, depending on the healing modality and the depth of the work being explored.

Can healing sessions replace therapy or medical treatment?

No. Spiritual healing is intended to complement your personal growth and wellbeing journey. It is not a substitute for professional medical, psychological, or psychiatric care.

Will one session be enough?

Every journey is unique. Some people experience significant clarity after a single session, while others benefit from ongoing healing work. Recommendations will be based on your individual needs.

Is everything discussed during the session confidential?

Absolutely. Your privacy, comfort, and trust are deeply respected. All sessions are conducted with complete confidentiality and compassion.

Can I book a session for a specific issue?

Yes. Whether you're navigating relationship challenges, emotional wounds, life transitions, recurring patterns, spiritual questions, or a desire for deeper self-understanding, sessions can be tailored to your intention.

How often should I book sessions?

This varies from person to person. Some clients book a single session for clarity, while others choose ongoing support as they move through deeper layers of healing and transformation.

How do I book a session?

Booking a session is simple. You can fill out the booking form on the website or connect directly via WhatsApp. Once your request is received, you'll be guided through the next steps, including selecting the right service, choosing a suitable time slot, and confirming your appointment.
