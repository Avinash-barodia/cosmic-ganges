Past Life Regression

Explore recurring patterns, emotional blocks, fears, and relationships that may have roots beyond your current lifetime. Past Life Regression helps uncover deeper insights, allowing you to understand your experiences from a broader soul perspective.

What Is This?

Past Life Regression is a gentle, therapeutic process that allows you to access and explore memories, experiences, and patterns from prior incarnations. It operates on the premise that our souls carry imprints from past lifetimes—both positive wisdom and unresolved traumas—that actively shape our current reality, behaviors, and relationships.

Under a guided, relaxed state, you will safely journey into your subconscious to witness these stories firsthand. By bringing these hidden narratives into the light of conscious awareness, we can understand the root causes of deeply ingrained fears, unexplainable physical symptoms, or repeating karmic cycles.

Who Is This For?

Repeating Patterns

You find yourself stuck in identical relationship or career loops despite your best efforts to change.

Unexplained Fears

You experience phobias, anxieties, or physical pains that have no clear medical or psychological origin in this life.

Soul Connections

You feel an intense, immediate, or complicated bond with someone and wish to understand your karmic history.

Deeper Purpose

You are seeking profound spiritual insight and a broader understanding of your soul's overarching journey.

What Happens In A Session?

Your session begins with a compassionate consultation where we discuss your intentions and any specific patterns you wish to explore. You are then guided into a deeply relaxed, meditative state using gentle visualization techniques. You remain entirely safe, conscious, and in control throughout the process.

As memories arise, Neelam will guide you through the experience, asking clarifying questions to help you gather important details and understand the context of the lifetime. Afterward, we gently transition back to waking consciousness and spend time integrating the experience, discussing how the insights apply directly to your current life.

Frequently Asked Questions

Will I lose control during the regression?

No, you will not lose control. You remain fully aware of your surroundings in the present moment while simultaneously observing the past. You can choose to stop the process at any time.

What if I can't be hypnotized?

Regression does not require deep clinical hypnosis. It simply requires a relaxed, focused state similar to deep meditation or daydreaming. Most people who are open and willing can easily access these memories.

What if I see something frightening?

Your subconscious mind acts as a protector and will only reveal what you are ready to process. If a difficult memory arises, you are guided to observe it from a detached, safe perspective rather than reliving the trauma.
