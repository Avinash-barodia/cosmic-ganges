The Analytical Foundation

Before stepping into the world of spiritual healing, Neelam Samnani spent nearly two decades building a career in computer science. Her foundation was rooted in logic, analysis, and problem-solving, always seeking understanding through observation and experience.

The Unanswered Questions

Over time, life presented questions that could not be answered through logic alone. Questions about purpose, emotional patterns, healing, and the deeper forces shaping human experiences led her on a path of self-discovery and spiritual exploration.

The Calling to Datia

This journey eventually brought her to Datia, a place known for its powerful spiritual energy and sacred traditions. What began as a personal search for answers gradually evolved into a deeper calling. Through years of study, practice, and inner work, Neelam immersed herself in healing modalities that helped her understand the emotional, energetic, and spiritual layers of life.

Science Meets Spirituality

What makes her approach unique is the balance she brings between intuition and grounded awareness. Her background in technology taught her to observe carefully, listen deeply, and seek genuine understanding rather than assumptions. Today, she carries the same thoughtful approach into every healing session.

A Sanctuary for Healing

Through Cosmic Ganges, Neelam guides individuals on journeys of self-discovery, healing, and transformation through Past Life Regression, Inner Child Healing, Akashic Records, Reiki, and Tarot Guidance. Over the years, she has supported hundreds of individuals navigating emotional challenges, recurring life patterns, relationship struggles, spiritual growth, and personal transformation.
