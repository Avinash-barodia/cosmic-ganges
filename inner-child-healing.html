Inner Child Healing

Reconnect with the younger parts of yourself that still carry unmet needs, emotional wounds, or limiting beliefs. Inner Child Healing helps create self-compassion, emotional freedom, and healthier patterns in your present life.

What Is This?

Inner Child Healing is a profound emotional integration process. We all have younger versions of ourselves living within our subconscious, holding onto the beliefs, fears, and unmet needs established during our formative years. When these parts of us feel unsafe, they react, causing emotional triggers and self-sabotaging behaviors in our adult lives.

Through guided visualization, deep listening, and compassionate presence, we journey inward to meet these younger selves. By providing the safety, validation, and love they never received, we allow these emotional wounds to finally close, returning you to a state of wholeness.

Who Is This For?

Emotional Triggers

You find yourself overreacting to situations or feeling disproportionately abandoned, rejected, or unseen.

People Pleasing

You struggle to set boundaries, constantly seeking external validation at the expense of your own needs.

Self-Sabotage

You undermine your own success, happiness, or relationships due to deeply ingrained feelings of unworthiness.

Inner Critic

You battle a harsh internal dialogue that judges your actions, appearance, or worth on a daily basis.

What Happens In A Session?

We start by identifying current triggers or emotional blocks you are experiencing. You will then be gently guided into a relaxed, meditative state where we can safely access your subconscious landscape. Together, we locate the younger part of you associated with the original wound.

Neelam facilitates a safe dialogue between your adult self and your inner child. You will have the opportunity to listen to their fears, offer reassurance, and symbolically release the heavy emotions they have been carrying. The session concludes with a powerful integration process, bringing your healed inner child back into the safety of the present moment.

Benefits

  • Experience deep emotional relief from childhood conditioning.
  • Develop healthier boundaries and stop people-pleasing.
  • Transform your harsh inner critic into a voice of self-compassion.
  • Improve the quality of your adult relationships by reducing emotional reactivity.

Frequently Asked Questions

Will I have to relive painful childhood memories?

The goal is not to relive trauma, but to rescue the part of you trapped in it. You are guided to observe from a place of safety and empowerment as your adult self.

What if I don't remember much of my childhood?

That is completely normal. The subconscious mind holds the emotional imprints even if the conscious mind has blocked the visual memories. We work directly with the feeling in the body.

How many sessions does this take?

Inner child work is an ongoing relationship, but clients often feel significant emotional shifts after a single deep-dive session. Ongoing sessions can address different ages and specific core wounds.
